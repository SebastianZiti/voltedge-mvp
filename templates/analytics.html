LoadForecast — domain service

The MVP trains a scikit-learn LinearRegression model on occupied charger telemetry. Features: time_index and hour_of_day. Target: power_kw. When fewer than five occupied readings exist, the service falls back to a mean baseline (MeanBaseline) so the dashboard always has a value.

The service maps to the Charging Operations Intelligence bounded context and the LoadForecastCalculated domain event. GET paths are pure queries (CQRS); publishing the forecast as a domain event happens via the explicit POST /api/analytics/forecast/publish endpoint.
